## PROCEDURAL TIMELINE

**After Filing:**

**Day 1:** File Petition with Court
- Pay filing fee (~$465-$500)
- Obtain hearing date (usually 30-45 days out)

**Days 1-7:** Publication and Notice
- Arrange publication in newspaper (3 weeks before hearing)
- Serve notice on all heirs at least 15 days before hearing

**Day of Hearing:** Court Hearing
- Appear in court
- Judge reviews petition
- If approved, Letters of Administration issued

**Post-Hearing:**
- File Inventory and Appraisal (within 4 months)
- Notify creditors (publish notice, mail to known creditors)
- Administer estate (pay debts, distribute assets)
- File final accounting and petition for distribution
